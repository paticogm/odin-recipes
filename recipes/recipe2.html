Instant Pot Vegan Cabbage Detox Soup

This simple vegan cabbage soup is perfect for a detox diet. It's a tasty recipe that's easy to make in your Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk
  1. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.
